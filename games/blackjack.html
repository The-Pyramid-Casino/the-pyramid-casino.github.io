How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Beat the dealer's hand to win.

Card Values

Number cards = face value, Face cards = 10, Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever is better).

Actions

Hit: Take another card
Stand: Keep current hand
Double: Double bet and take one card

Winning

Blackjack (21 with 2 cards) pays 3:2. Regular wins pay 1:1. Ties are a push.
